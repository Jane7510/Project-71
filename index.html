Campus Life

Classrooms

Classroom furniture is suitable for independent as well as group work. All students have a personal space in the classroom to keep their belongings. The limited number of students in each class helps teachers to give individual attention to the learning needs.



Libraries

The school has two Library-cum-Reading Rooms, with a wide selection of reading materials, reference books and manuals for students as well as teachers. A large collection of videos and other digital resources classified and catalogued for different age-groups and subject areas is an addendum to the treasure of knowledge in the print format. Children are encouraged to subscribe to books of their choice from Primary level itself.



Laboratories

Laboratories for Physics, Chemistry, Biology and Mathematics have advanced digital equipments. The digital microscope is the special feature of the Biology Lab. This microscope can be connected to computer system through cable interface. All-in-one, touch enabled computers are connected to these digital microscopes. Students can view the objects from view finder or watch them on the computer screen. As these are touchscreen computers, students can directly zoom in to get the desired information about the object on screen.



Resource Room

The school helps students, who need strengthening of concepts, by offering revision and practice work during the school days and weekends to cope with the grade-level requirements and eventually become self-reliant. The school thoroughly discourages students from going for tuitions.

The school also identifies and provides remedial support to the students with special learning needs through its Resource Room that is equipped with learning materials and manipulatives. A special educator is available to monitor and help with the remediation programme.

Awareness and sensitisation programme for parents and teachers are held at frequent intervals to support the students in inclusive classrooms and at home.



Information Technology Labs

The two Information Technology labs are equipped with 80 latest, all-in-one, touch screen computer systems. These computers are equipped with the latest Windows 8.1 and Windows 10 Operating System. They can run any kind of complex program such as, animation software, web designing software, programming language, 3D software, etc.

The computers have unlimited internet connectivity and are interconnected through a network, which allows for easy access to projects and programmes created during the practical sessions to showcase in classes.



Indoor And Outdoor

As the saying goes, ‘A healthy mind in a healthy body', EPS believes in giving its students enough opportunities to play and participate in sporting events. Students play throwball, basketball, cricket, badminton, table tennis and track & field sports also for all-round development. They are encouraged and prepared to participate in interschool sports events in and around Mysuru.



Music And Dance

There is music in our hearts and rhythm in our bodies. This allows children to actively take to music or dance and use it to enhance their learning. This also gives them an opportunity to identify their own abilities and nurture them as an interesting leisure activity.



Art and Crafts Room

The students love to spend time in the Art and Craft rooms. They use their nimble fingers to bring pictures to life and make various objects with different material, whether it is origami or other items like paper flowers, pen holders, greeting cards, bookmarks, etc.



School Transport

The school currently has a fleet of school buses, owned and operated independently by the school. School transport facility is available to most parts of Mysuru.

Every bus has an attendant who accompanies the children during the trips to and from school.

Every bus is fitted with a GPS device and CCTV, as per the government guidelines.

Parents receive an SMS alert every time the bus leaves the school for pickup or drop of the children.



Infirmary

The school has a 9-bed infirmary set-up that provides first aid to students in need, under the supervision of a full-time staff nurse. The school is also tied up with a few hospitals in Mysuru and conducts regular health checkup for students.

Activities And Fun

STEM Fest

STEM Fest

STEM is the acronym for Science, Technology, Engineering and Mathematics. Today’s students decide the future of our country and it is important that our country remains competitive in fields of science, technology and medicine. Hence, STEM is the best way to ensure that students are exposed to mathematics, science and technology throughout their educational career. The students of EPS are extremely curious and impressionable, so instilling an interest at an early age could spark a lasting desire to pursue a career in any of these fields. Excel Public School incorporates the STEM concepts in the teaching-learning experience of the learners from the Secondary stage.



Kids Dhamaal

Kids Dhamaal

Every year in the month of September, the school hosts a Kids Dhamaal. During that time, there are many stalls of food and games! The class teachers give you coupons to use for playing games and eating food. There is also a Lucky Dip. Kids Dhamaal is the favourite time of the academic year to a humongous amount of students(it's mine too!). You are allowed to come with your parents and family.

Kannada Rajyotsava

Every year in the month of November, the school celebrates Kannada Rajyotsava with great pomp and show. The teachers and students get together and practice skits, dances and songs in Kannada. In the evening, we perform in front of our friends and family.

About EPS And Its Vision And Mission

  • About Eps

    Excel Public School, Mysuru, is the realisation of the dream of the eminent educationist and visionary, Prof. M H Dhananjay and the founder of Excelsoft Technologies, Mr D Sudhanva the MD and CEO of Excelsoft, a global leader in innovative technology-based solutions for education and training. The School is run by Excel Education and E-Learning Trust, a non-profitable, charitable trust headquartered in Mysuru.

    Since its humble inception in 2009, Excel Public School (EPS) has grown from being just another school in the city’s firmament, to an institution that is recognised for bringing about a transformation in the school education, synchronous with the contemporary global standards.

    The founding principles of the school are clearly laid out to serve as the guiding light for all the stakeholders; they are clearly communicated and practiced to create and sustain an EPS culture among the members of the staff, students and parents. Emphasis is laid on value systems to create self-motivated, self-regulated individuals through various opportunities at school. This transforms them into sensitive and sensible individuals, empowered to make informed decisions and become the change agents for a better tomorrow.

    The instructional strategies at Excel Public School are dynamic, contemporary and primarily constructivist, where technology plays the role of an effective enabler. Learning happens in a heterogeneous and inclusive ecosystem.

  • Vision And Mission

    Vision

    Empowering young minds towards a progressive and humane society.

    Mission

    EPS strives to create a unique atmosphere for learning, with a creative and effective integration of technology across the whole curriculum, in a happy, caring, secure and value-based environment, to prepare a generation of young men and women, who are intellectually sharp, physically sound, socially responsible, morally upright and emotionally balanced.
